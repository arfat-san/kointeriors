A seasoned Designer and Planner with a rich twelve-year career in Mumbai. My journey has been marked by successful collaborations with leading architects & interior designers, allowing me to transform concepts into stunning realities.

As a self-professed "maker at heart," I am constantly exploring new ways to push the boundaries of design. I believe that thoughtful, well-executed interiors have the power to significantly enhance the quality of life, and I am dedicated to delivering the most effective and elegant solutions for every project.

In my role, I see design not just as a profession, but as a vehicle for positive change. Whether it's a cozy home or a dynamic office space, I strive to create environments that inspire and uplift those who inhabit them.
